## The 5-Step AI Marketing Framework

### Step 1: Define Your Value Ladder

Before any marketing happens, map your offers from free to premium. Russell Brunson's Value Ladder framework structures this as:

- **Lead Magnet** (free): A PDF guide, checklist, or mini-course that solves one specific problem
- **Tripwire** ($7–$27): A low-cost entry product that delivers immediate value
- **Core Offer** ($47–$297): Your main product or service
- **Backend** ($500+): Premium coaching, consulting, or done-for-you services

Tell your AI tool: "Apply Russell Brunson's Value Ladder to my business. My product is [X], my ideal customer struggles with [Y], and my current average sale is [Z]." The output will be a specific ladder — not generic advice.

### Step 2: Build Your Launch Sequence

Jeff Walker's Product Launch Formula breaks product launches into four phases: Pre-Pre-Launch, Pre-Launch, Launch, and Post-Launch. Each phase has specific content and psychological triggers.

Instead of guessing what to say when, give AI the PLF framework and your product details. It will generate:

- Pre-launch email sequences with specific hooks
- Social proof integration points
- Scarcity and urgency triggers timed to your launch calendar
- Post-launch follow-up for non-buyers

### Step 3: Write Copy That Converts

Jim Edwards' Copywriting Secrets framework provides templates for headlines, bullets, stories, and calls to action. Combined with AI, you can generate dozens of copy variations in minutes — then test the best performers.

The key insight: AI with a copywriting framework writes better copy than AI without one. The framework constrains the output to patterns that are proven to convert.

### Step 4: Create Content at Scale

Nicolas Cole's framework for online writing turns expertise into consistent, engaging content. The system covers:

- Content ideation from your expertise (not trending topics)
- Hook structures that stop the scroll
- Format templates that match each platform
- Repurposing workflows: one piece becomes five

AI handles the production. The framework handles the strategy.

### Step 5: Drive Targeted Traffic

Combine your Value Ladder with traffic systems that match each level. Free content attracts cold traffic. Tripwire offers convert warm traffic. Email sequences nurture leads to your core offer.

AI can analyze your traffic sources, identify your highest-converting channels, and generate platform-specific content for each one — all within a proven traffic framework.
